h1. SapperWeb

This is a simple html5 sapper game.

h2. Playing

Click tiles to open. Numbers on backside of tiles will help you to find mines, which you can mark with flags. 
To mark a tile you have to click on Flag on the top panel and then click to the tile you want to mark.
To unflag a tile just click to it again.
To finish the game click to blue triangle, if your pattern is good - game will be won.
